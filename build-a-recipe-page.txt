    <h1>Molokhia Recipe</h1>
    <p>
      Molokhia (Jute Mallow) – fresh or frozen
Broth – chicken, beef, or vegetable
Garlic – minced
Coriander – ground or fresh
Butter or Olive Oil
Salt
Black Pepper
Lemon Juice (optional)
Protein (optional) – chicken, beef, or seafood
</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>1 bunch fresh molokhia (or 200g frozen molokhia)</li>
<li>4 cups chicken broth (or beef broth)</li>
<li>2-3 cloves garlic, minced</li>
<li>1 tablespoon coriander (ground or fresh, chopped)</li>
</ul>
<li>2 tablespoons butter or olive oil</li>
<li>1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)</li>
<li>1 teaspoon black pepper</li>
<li>Juice of 1 lemon (optional, for serving)</li>
<li>1 whole chicken or beef chunks (optional, for a richer flavor)</li>
<h2>Instructions</h2>
<ol>
<li>Prepare the Molokhia:
If using fresh molokhia, remove the leaves from the stems and finely chop them. If using frozen, let it thaw slightly.</li>
<li>Prepare the Broth:
Boil chicken or beef with onions, bay leaves, and spices to make a flavorful broth. Strain and keep it hot.</li>
<li>Sauté Garlic and Coriander:

In a pan, heat butter or oil, add minced garlic, and cook until golden. Add coriander and stir for a few seconds.</li>
<li>Cook the Molokhia:

Add the molokhia to the broth, stirring continuously. Simmer for 5-7 minutes on low heat (do not overcook).</li>
<li>Final Touches:

Add salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon. Serve with rice or pita bread, and optionally with chicken or beef on the side.</li>
